GYM

Within our clubs we've developed a brand new type of gym that integrates numerous types of training in a really social, open-plan layout. It’s divided into specialised zones – we have areas for cardio, free weights, weights machines, functional training and more.

With so much choice, it’s really easy to keep your workout interesting and fresh at ROCOFIT


TENNIS COURTS & FACILITIES

Given our love of tennis, it’s no surprise that our facilities are second to none. We’ve got the world’s largest team of qualified coaches, as well as 850 match-quality courts, both indoors and outdoors. It means you can have a lesson, play a match or practice your serve all year round, whatever the weather – and you can book courts in advance really quickly with our app too.

Our clubs are also home to vibrant communities of tennis-lovers, with competitions, matches and social events making it easy to find new people to play, whatever your level.


SWIMMING POOL & SPA FACILITIES

Here at ROCOFIT, we offer a wide range of facilities and services for the whole family. With both indoor and outdoor pools at the majority of our clubs, you can look forward to swimming all year round, regardless of the weather. Our pools are suitable for both intensive training and relaxing play-time for the kids. Plus, with special shallow, splash pools at most clubs and trained lifeguards on constant watch during family swim sessions, allow your kids to enjoy the water with complete peace of mind.

Once you’re out of the pool, relax in our state-of-the-art spas, complete with sauna, steam-room and whirlpools depending on your club.
